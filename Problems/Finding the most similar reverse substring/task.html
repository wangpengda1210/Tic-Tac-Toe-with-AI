Finding the most similar reverse substring

Given two strings of equal length (denoted as \( s \) and \( t \)) and an integer \( k \). Write a program that finds the index \( i \) such that the Hamming distance between \(reverse(s[i:i+k])\) and \(t[i:i+k]\) is the minimal.

Input: Two strings of equal length and an integer \( k \).

Output: Two numbers: the first is the index \( i \) satisfying the conditions above, the second is the Hamming distance between corresponding substrings. If there are several such indexes, print the minimal one.


Sample Input:
123456789
654654654
3

Sample Output:
3 0


Sample Input:
111001
011000
4

Sample Output:
0 1



Memory limit: 256 MB
Time limit: 8 seconds
